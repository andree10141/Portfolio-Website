About

Make every shoot count.

When it comes to creativity I believe that I have an eye to make my vision come through. I do not let anything stop me from achieving my goals. When I set out to do something I am the type of person to get it done.

As a self-taught photographer and digital editor. Everything that I have learned has come from trial and error. I do not let myself be hindered by ignorance. I take challenges head on and allow myself to ask for help when needed from others. I also have worked in a professional theater space which has allowed me to learn the basics i n working in a demanding fast-paced environment. The skills that I cherish are how to light a subject, how to set up theatrical lighting equipment in any way, and how to work in a stressful environment. Everything has molded me into who I am.

I do not set limits on what I can do with the resources given. I challenge myself to raise the bar no matter the circumstances. If I only have one light, I will make it work. That is my kind of work ethic. To do the absolute most.
